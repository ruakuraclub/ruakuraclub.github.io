Membership at RCC

An active membership is essential for the well-being of the club.

Membership benefits
  • Access to great facilities for members to enjoy
    • A bar with a fantastic range of craft beers - as good as anywhere in Hamilton
    • Squash, Tennis and Swimming Pool available
    • Facilities for hire - meetings or functions, with catering available
  • Always a welcoming and friendly vibe - come in and unwind after work.
  • Reciprocal visiting rights to over 300 clubs throughout New Zealand

We're always trying to maintain an active and positive atmosphere in our club community, and will regularly run events and competitions throughout the year.

If you have any questions or suggestions, please feel free to contact us at any time.

Spread the word! Please do your best to help grow the club by showing relatives, friends and colleagues what a great place we have here.
