Ruakura Club Swimming Pool

The RCC provides a 25 metre 6 lane outdoor pool for members on payment of an annual key fee.

The pool is open daily from 6 am to 10 pm during the swimming season.

The swimming season is Labour weekend through to Easter weekend the following year.

From Monday to Friday between 12 noon and 2 pm there will be lap/lane swimming only.

A copy of the pool rules and signup form are available here.

Pool Fees

  • New Members: $100 (1 wrist band)
  • Renewal: $80 (1 wrist band)
  • Extra wrist bands: $30 each
  • Please note as per Clubs NZ requirements, Swimming club members must also be current members of Ruakura Club

Enquiries

  • Office Phone: 027 622 9309
  • Email: rcc.admin@ruakura-club.co.nz

Have fun this summer. Be SAFE and remember every swimmer over 5 yrs of age must have a wrist band on to use the pool.

Please report persons using the pool without wrist bands or entering illegally.
