Swimming Pool

The RCC provides a 25 meter 6 lane swimming pool for members on payment of an annual key fee.

The pool is open daily from 6 am to 10 pm during the swimming season.

The swimming season is Labour weekend through to Easter weekend the following year.

From Monday to Friday between 12 noon and 2 pm there will be lap/lane swimming only.

A copy of the pool rules and signup form are available here.

Pool Fees

  • New Member: $100 (1 wrist band)
  • Renewal: $80 (1 wrist band)
  • Extra wrist bands $30 each

Have fun this summer. Be SAFE and remember that to use the pool every swimmer over 5 yrs of age must have a wrist band on to use the pool. Please report persons using the pool without wrist bands or entering illegally.
