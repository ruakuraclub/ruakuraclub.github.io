Facilities for Hire

The club is available for conference and function hire

Conference Services and Facilities

We are able to offer facilities and assistance for a full conference.

Features:

  • Projector
  • Sound System
  • Catering available by arrangement

Hirage:

  • Half Day: $80.00 plus GST
  • Full Day: $100.00 plus GST

Function/Party Facilities

The Club & Bar including outdoor BBQ area is available over the weekends for that special celebration of Wedding, Birthday, Reunion or just to get together. (Highly recommended that the facility be viewed)

Hirage:

  • $135.00 plus $85.00 cleaning fee
  • Special License (if not a Financial Member of the Club) from $65.00
    Please allow a minimum of 20 working days when lodging a special license application.
