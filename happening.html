Winter trivia quiz has begun on Thursday evenings every fortnight starting from the 10th May, trivia starts at 7 PM and runs until 9 PM.

Teams of up to 6, register interest with Pete Dobbie via email dobbiep@xtra.co.nz.

On the 22nd of June, come celebrate the (almost) shortest day of the year with us at the club at our Mid-winter Christmas Dinner in aid of New Lives Animal Rescue.

President's message

Welcome to the New Membership Year of the Ruakura Campus Club and I hope you will re-join this year, using the attached renewal form. An active membership is essential for the well-being of the club. Why re-join? Well:

  • Access to great facilities for members to enjoy
    • A bar with a fantastic range of craft beers – as good as anywhere in Hamilton
    • Squash, tennis and swimming available
    • Facilities for hire - meetings or functions, with catering available
  • Always a friendly and welcoming vibe – it’s great to see a diverse range of folk unwinding together after work. Network or just generally have a laugh – we don’t mind!
  • Furthermore, Membership fees have not increased. This is due to a fantastic effort by the outgoing Committee, who have done a great job managing the club in an increasingly difficult financial environment.
I have just one request: SPREAD THE WORD! Slowly, new folk are discovering the hidden treasure that is Ruakura Club. Please do your best to help us grow even quicker by showing relatives, friends or colleagues what a great place we have. With a thriving and growing membership the Committee will be able to make the place even better. And renew your membership by 30th November to receive a discount.

Mark Shepherd
