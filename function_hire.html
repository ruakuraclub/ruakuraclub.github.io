Hire Facilities

Management and Executive welcome members, affiliated members and visitors to the Club.

Anyone wanting to arrange a special function or a business conference is to liaise direct with the club:

  • Office phone: 027 622 9309
  • Email: rcc.admin@ruakura-club.co.nz

Our facilities are ideal for the business entrepreneur who wants to take their staff on a team building exercise.

Conference Services and Facilities

We are able to offer facilities that can provide and assist for your needs for a full conference.

Catering requirements and provide all your audio-visual requirements and presentation equipment that you may need.

Hirage:

  • Half Day: $80.00 plus GST
  • Full Day: $100.00 plus GST

Club Facility for a Function

The Club & Bar including Sail BBQ area is available over the weekends for that special celebration of Wedding, Birthday, Reunion or just to get together. (Highly recommended that the facility be viewed)

Hirage:

$135.00 plus $85.00 cleaning fee

Special License required if not a Financial Member of the RCC from $65.00. Please allow a minimum of 20 clear working days when lodging a special license application.
