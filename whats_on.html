Covid-19/Coronavirus

The club will continue to operate unless otherwise advised by the Ministry of Health.

The following are some of the steps we are taking to increase your safety:

  • Increased sanitation of all work and public spaces
  • Removal of cutlery from all tables, these will be delivered with your meal
  • Removal of 'self-service' sauce and cutlery stations
  • Increased use of gloves, including in acceptance of deliveries
