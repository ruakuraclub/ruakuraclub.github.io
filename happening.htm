Tennis Club  

 

The RCC maintains 2 asphalt tennis courts for Tennis Club members use. The fees are $20 per year which includes a key. For convenience there is a booking sheet in the Club foyer, for one hour game slots.

 

A few of the Club members organise an Annual Mixed Doubles Tournament, which runs over February and March. It is very popular and well contested so there are a range of grades to cover all levels of play.

 

New players are encouraged to use this tournament to appreciate the fun of playing tennis”

 

Tennis Fees: New member $20 + $15 for a court key. Renewal $20 (and key exchanged at no cost)

 

 

The Swimming Pool is now closed for the season

 

Swimming  The Ruakura Campus Club provides a 25 meter 6 lane swimming pool for members on payment of an annual key fee.

 

The swimming pool is open daily from 6 am to 10 pm.

 

From Monday to Friday between 12 noon and 2 pm there will be lap/lane swimming only.

 

The swimming season is Labour weekend through to Easter weekend the following year.

  

Swimming Pool Fees  

New Member $75 (2 wrist bands)  Renewal $75 (2 wrist bands) $15 for a key.  Key renewed at no cost.

 

Extra wrist bands $20 each

 

Please report persons using the Pool without wrist bands or illegal entry.
