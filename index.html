A small club with a big heart

Founded in 1946 and located in the heart of Waikato Innovation Park, Ruakura Club is a central social hub for the surrounding campus, Waikato University and local community

Hosting a lovely patronage of interesting regulars, beer enthusiasts, and local families. We have a solid beer selection that rotates regularly, featuring some of the top craft beers from brewers around New Zealand.

Come on down to enjoy our brews, our delicious food, and soak up the fantastic atmosphere!
